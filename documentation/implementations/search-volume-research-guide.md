# Search Volume Research Guide for Mexico City Sports

## Google Search Console Setup

### Step 1: Access Your Search Console
1. Go to [search.google.com/search-console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Add your property: `https://thepickleco.com`
3. Verify ownership (DNS or HTML tag)

### Step 2: Research Keywords
1. Go to **Performance** tab
2. Click **"New"** → **"Search"**
3. Enter these keywords to check current performance:

#### Pickleball Keywords:
- pickleball Mexico City
- clases de pickleball CDMX
- donde jugar pickleball Mexico City
- pickleball para principiantes CDMX
- torneos de pickleball Mexico City

#### Padel Keywords:
- padel Mexico City
- clases de padel CDMX
- donde jugar padel Mexico City
- canchas de padel CDMX
- torneos de padel Mexico City

#### Tennis Keywords:
- tennis Mexico City

### Step 3: Analyze Results
- **Impressions:** How many times your site appeared in search
- **Clicks:** How many people clicked through
- **CTR:** Click-through rate
- **Position:** Average ranking position

## Alternative Tools (Paid)

### 1. SEMrush (Recommended)
- **Cost:** $119/month
- **Best for:** Comprehensive keyword research
- **Features:** Search volume, competition, keyword difficulty

### 2. Ahrefs
- **Cost:** $99/month
- **Best for:** Detailed SEO analysis
- **Features:** Search volume, backlink analysis, competitor research

### 3. Google Keyword Planner (Free)
- **Cost:** Free (requires Google Ads account)
- **Best for:** Basic search volume data
- **Features:** Monthly search volume, competition level
